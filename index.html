About Us

A Drishti Foundation, a non-profit Organization , is dedicated towards making India blind-free. With the help of our NGOs, we are providing free facilitites and eye care appliances to the needy people and we are moving one step closer to our goal every day. Together, we believe, we can do it, because everyone has the right to see the world.

our Vision

To eradicate blindness and eye disease from the marginalized sections of society. The quality of eye care and medical technology available in the market, though increasing, is still beyond the reach of most affected people in the country.
